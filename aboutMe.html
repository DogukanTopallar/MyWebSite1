ABOUT ME

Who Am I?

I'm Doğukan Topallar. I was born on August 31, 2000. I completed my primary education in Balikesir/Erdek. I completed my secondary and high school education in Tekirdağ/Çorlu. I played professional basketball in the Banvit Basketball Club and Çorlu Municipality Youth and Sports Club, which started in Erdek 18 Eylül Primary School. I am currently playing basketball in Unilig in the University Basketball Team.

My interest in electronics started at a very young age by dismantling my toy cars :) I started by studying Electronics and Communication Engineering at university. I started to study Computer Engineering at the same time to meet the requirements of the industry and improve myself in software areas. You can find detailed information about my competencies and fields of study from the "open my cv" tab on my home page.
